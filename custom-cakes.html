Delivery & Pickup Information

We take the utmost care to ensure your bespoke cakes arrive safely and beautifully. Review our delivery guidelines below.

Delivery Service

We proudly serve Panjim, Merces, Santa Cruz, Taleigao, Porvorim, Bambolim, and Old Goa.

Delivery fees are calculated based on distance from our bakery in Merces. To ensure safe transport, all multi-tier cakes are delivered personally by our team.

Store Pickup

Available at The Shah Harmony Co op Housing Society Ltd, Moloca Merces.

Please bring a flat-bottom vehicle with AC. Pickups must be scheduled during our business hours (Mon-Sat: 9am - 6pm).

Order Timeline: Please provide at least 3-5 days notice for custom celebration cakes.
