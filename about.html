A Journey of Passion

My journey began in 2016. Looking for the flexibility of working from home while raising my two young sons, I decided to turn a deep-seated passion into something more. It started simply—baking cupcakes and teacakes for family and close friends.

What began as a small hobby quickly grew. Word of mouth spread, and soon I was expanding into customized celebration cakes. The joy of being a part of someone's special occasion fueled my ambition, turning my personal passion into a growing, heartfelt bakery business right here in Goa.

The Turning Point

"I wanted to create treats that bring joy without compromising on wellness."

As the business grew, so did my vision. A pivotal moment came when I experimented with a wheat flour cake, sweetened entirely with jaggery and enriched with superseeds. My mother, who is diabetic, was able to enjoy this cake comfortably without any health concerns. Seeing her savor a dessert guilt-free inspired a new mission for The Bake Shop: creating premium baked goods that beautifully combine taste, indulgence, and wellness.

Meet Latifa

"There is no failure, only feedback."

Transitioning from a homemaker to an entrepreneur wasn't easy, but my passion for baking kept me going. I've always had a keen interest in psychology and personal growth, which deeply influences how I run my business today.

For me, baking isn't just about mixing ingredients; it's about connecting with people, listening to their needs, and ensuring their well-being. Every cake is a reflection of my commitment to crafting something truly special for my customers.
